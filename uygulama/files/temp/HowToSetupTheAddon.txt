How to setup the Addon:
1.	Download both the CustomPlanetAddon.zip and the CustomPlanetImages.zip (Depending on your addon version, the image folder will be named a bit differently)
2.	Extract the CustomPlanetImages.zip, so that it is a normal folder (The folder is nearly 4 GB big, so put it where you have space on your hard drive.)
3.	Keep the CustomPlanetAddon.zip as it is, and install it in Blender in the user preferences as an addon (As you would do with all addons. More here: https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/editors/preferences/addons.html).
4.	Don’t forget to also activate it.
5.	In Blender you can now press the N key, to open the sidebar. There you will find a new tab called Custom Planet.
6.      Pick the path of your image folder.

You are now ready to create planets, by pressing the new button.
